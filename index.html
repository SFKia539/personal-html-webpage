Welcome to my Webpage about Blacktip reef sharks

  • Size: They only grow to be four feet long, they are not very large sharks.
  • Diet: Blacktip reef sharks are daytime hunters that feed on, crustaceans, reef fish and cephalopods.

This is the black tip reef shark, they are known for the black tip on the top of there fin. This makes them easyly identified compared to other species of sharks.
